THE CENTRAL PARK PUNE



#1 Best Value of 2,187 places to stay in Pune Charming hotel offering consistently good food, both vegetarian and non-vegetarian. Praised for its excellent service, spacious rooms, and comfortable beds. Conveniently located near the Pune railway station. A 5-minute walk from Pune Railway Station, The Central Park offers non-smoking rooms with a cable TV. This hotel features a rooftop swimming pool, fitness centre and 4 dining options. Free parking are provided. The Central Park is a 10-minute walk from the lively M.G. Road and the Central Business District. Pune Airport is a 15-minute drive from the hotel. Rooms at The Central Park are fitted with modern interiors and warm lighting. Each is equipped with a tea/coffee maker and private bathroom with shower facilities. Guests can enjoy body treatments at the spa, or spend some quiet time in the library. The hotel provides car rental and currency exchange services. Travel bookings can be made at the tour desk. The rooftop Smoke on the Water restaurant serves grilled dishes, while waffles and sandwiches are available at Flap Jack. Other dining choices include refreshing drinks at Restolounge Zanzibar.

THE COCOON



#2 Best Value of 2,187 places to stay in Pune Spacious hotel offering excellent amenities, including separate living areas, balconies and kitchen facilities. Notable for its efficient room service, impressive room maintenance, and fine dining. Ideal for working guests, with a conveniently close location to Magarpatta City. Situated in Pune, 8.8 km from Darshan Museum, Cocoon Hotel features accommodation with a fitness centre, free private parking, a restaurant and a bar. This 3-star hotel offers room service, a 24-hour front desk and free WiFi. The accommodation provides an ATM, a concierge service and organising tours for guests. At the hotel the rooms include air conditioning, a desk, a flat-screen TV, a private bathroom, bed linen, towels and a balcony with a city view. All rooms will provide guests with a wardrobe and a kettle. Breakfast is available each morning, and includes buffet, continental and Full English/Irish options. Aga Khan Palace is 9.1 km from Cocoon Hotel, while Pune Railway Station is 9.4 km from the property. The nearest airport is Pune International Airport, 10 km from the accommodation.

The Corinthians Resort & Club



Set in Pune, 11 km from Darshan Museum, The Corinthians Resort & Club offers accommodation with a fitness centre, free private parking, a garden and a restaurant. This 5-star resort offers room service and a concierge service. The accommodation provides a 24-hour front desk, airport transfers, a kids' club and free WiFi throughout the property. The resort will provide guests with air-conditioned rooms with a desk, a kettle, a minibar, a safety deposit box, a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom with a shower. At The Corinthians Resort & Club every room has bed linen and towels. The daily breakfast offers buffet, continental or vegetarian options. The accommodation offers an outdoor pool. You can play table tennis at The Corinthians Resort & Club, and bike hire and car hire are available. Pune Railway Station is 12 km from the resort, while Raja Dinkar Kelkar Museum is 12 km away. The nearest airport is Pune International Airport, 16 km from The Corinthians Resort & Club.

Sayaji hotel



Sayaji Pune Hotel offers 5-star accommodation with a large, outdoor pool and 2 dining options. It provides free Wi-Fi and has a tour desk which helps guests organise excursions to tourist attractions. The spacious air-conditioned rooms feature a comfortable seating area and an en suite bathroom. They are equipped with a flat-screen satellite TV, a safe and a minibar. Portico restaurant serves local, Chinese and Western dishes. Turque Poolside Lounge provides delicious Asian dishes. There is also a bar on site. Sayaji Hotel is about 22 km from Pune International Airport. Balewadi Stadium is only 4.4 km away, while Hinjewadi is 5.2 km from the hotel.
